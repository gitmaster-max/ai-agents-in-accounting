Claude Code Skill

Audit Perspectives

A STORM-style (Shao et al., NAACL 2024), multi-stakeholder method for audit questions. It runs any audit question through every layer of an engagement team — Staff through Partner, Core Audit through IT, Tax, and Advisory — plus external stakeholders like the PCAOB, AICPA, regulators, and investors.

Then it maps where the views conflict and synthesizes a practical recommendation specific enough to act on.

Financial Statement Intelligence

Upload a raw trial balance and get a department-by-department readiness assessment for financial statement preparation — Accounts Payable and Receivable, through the financial statement close process, to the CFO, end to end.

Every perspective is grounded in deterministic diagnostics computed from the TB — contra balances, stale suspense accounts, missing accruals — and rolls up into a red/amber/green readiness scorecard.
